Student Services

Fanshawe Studnet Union (FSU)

The Fanshawe Student Union (FSU) is the voice of the student body here at Fanshawe. They are the group that plans and runs events at the college ns well as runs other systems such as the FanCARD, Your FanCARD is your main ID on campus as well as a few places around London. Not only is it your ID but it also serves as your card for meal plan payment. The FSU desk is located in the student center on the main London campus for anyone who wants to pop in and ask further questions to them.

Food Services

Oasis is your main area for getting food off your meal plan. There are a few other options on campus but Oasis is the most popular for their wide variety of food choices. Containing a small Starbucks shop, a mexican foods station, a station for pasta and stir fry and a station for all day breakfast and other assorted food items. It also has other typical items you would find in corner stores such as drink fridges and shelves or assorted chocolates and other snacks.

Student Wellness Center

The student wellness center is the best place on campus to go for fitness and other activities. The wellness facilities come complete with a weight room and a full sized gymnasium as well as many sports that come with it. A few sports that Fanshawe offers include cross country, ball and floor hockey, dodgeball, baseball and many others. The wellness center also offers other areas such as a faith and spiritual room, quiet rooms and group rooms as well as a medical clinic and counseling.
